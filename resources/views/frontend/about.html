Bespoke Window Furnishings

With over 40 years of industry expertise, we specialize in creating custom window furnishings that meet the highest standards.

Our extensive range of high-quality products and exceptional customer service are designed to exceed expectations.

By eliminating the middleman and working directly with the manufacturer, we’re able to offer highly competitive prices. Our state of the art curtain factory, located in Delhi NCR, allows us to maintain strict quality control and optimize production speed.

Confident in our skilled manufacturing team, we back all our in-house products with a 1-year warranty. Plus, our efficient process ensures fast delivery, with an average turnaround time of just 10 -12 days —significantly quicker than unorganised shops.

Make in India

With a strong commitment to customer service, top-tier manufacturing, easy selection and  installation, and highly competitive prices, you can trust that your order is crafted by some of India’s most skilled and experienced curtain makers. 

Drawing on this multi-generational expertise, we manufacture our curtains locally, partnering with some of the finest mills across the country providing the highest quality fabrics. 

Our impressive range of materials includes stylish borders, elegant stitching styles and robust and high quality hardware. While our selection is vast, we never compromise on quality or affordability.

We even cater to commercial and hospitality projects

Elevate the aesthetics of commercial, residential, and hospitality spaces with our carefully curated collections.

Why settle for dull office interiors?

Our stunning commercial furnishings bring a fresh, modern appeal to work environments.

For hotels, we offer luxurious designs, including 100% blackout curtains to ensure ultimate privacy and comfort for guests. In office spaces, choose from a variety of blinds such as zebra, cellular, blackout, and sheer, tailored to balance style and functionality.
